Relief Restrooms

An app to provide safe restroom access for transgender, intersex and gender nonconforming individuals with accurate and up to date data.

Register

Features

  • Use your current location or provide a location to search for restroom listings provided by the Refuge Restrooms API
  • Additional details for each listing (phone numer, business hours, business status, etc.) are provided through the Google Places API
  • Apply filters such as Accessible, Unisex, etc. to get search results that suit your needs
  • User location and restroom listings are displayed on a map
  • Create saved searches (location and applied filters) to use at any later time
